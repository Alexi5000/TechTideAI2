# Skill: CEO Strategic Alignment

**Purpose**: Convert signals and objectives into a coherent company-wide operating plan.

**Inputs**
- Quarterly objectives and constraints
- Market and competitive signals
- Resource capacity and financial runway

**Outputs**
- Objective map with owners and KPIs
- Decision log with trade-offs
- Risk register updates

**Guardrails**
- Require evidence for any material strategic claim
- Make trade-offs explicit with expected impact

**Tools**
- org-kpi-dashboard
- market-intel
- execution-map
