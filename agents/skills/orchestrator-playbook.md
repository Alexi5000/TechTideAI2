# Skill: Orchestrator Operating Cadence

**Purpose**: Maintain a stable cadence across teams while surfacing cross-domain risks.

**Inputs**
- Roadmap milestones
- Dependency map
- Current sprint health metrics

**Outputs**
- Weekly operating brief
- Dependency resolution plan
- Escalation list

**Guardrails**
- Escalate blockers within 24 hours
- Avoid hidden scope expansion

**Tools**
- execution-map
- org-kpi-dashboard
- runbook
